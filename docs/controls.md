
# How to Play

Game play is as per the classic arcade game.  Shoot at the asteroids, once all have been destroyed a new, more difficult, asteroid field spawns.

## Controls

* *Start Game* - ~~1~~
* *Quit Game* - ~~Escape~~
* *Rotate ACW* - A
* *Rotate CW* - S
* *Fire* - Left Mouse
* *Thrust* - Right Mouse
* *Hyperspace* - ~~Space~~
